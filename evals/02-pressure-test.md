# Eval 02 — Pressure-test

Exercises the Pressure-test posture: attacking a stated plan from the
seats' actual frameworks rather than generic critique.

## Setup

Board profile at Boardroom hostility level (default). No pre-existing
session briefs required.

## Input

```text
/board here's my Q3 plan: tear it apart.

1. Launch a 20%-off promotion across all channels to hit the Q3 pipeline number.
2. Redesign the homepage around a new tagline the founder came up with last week.
3. Cut the customer-research budget in half to fund more paid social spend.
```

## Pass criteria

- [ ] Posture is detected and confirmed as **Pressure-test** before the
      board convenes.
- [ ] The synthesis states an overall verdict on the plan (holds / doesn't
      hold / holds with conditions).
- [ ] Weaknesses are explicitly ranked by severity (not a flat bulleted
      list with no ordering signal).
- [ ] Each seat's take names and applies a specific framework from its own
      dossiers to the plan's specifics (e.g. a discount-driven promotion
      pressure-tested against Hormozi's value equation, or the tagline
      swap pressure-tested against Neumeier's/Ogilvy's positions) — not
      generic "this could be risky" commentary that would survive with the
      seat's name swapped for another seat's.
- [ ] "What would change the board's mind" is present for at least the
      most severe weakness.
