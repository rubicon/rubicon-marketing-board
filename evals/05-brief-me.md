# Eval 05 — Brief me

Exercises the Brief me posture: teaching a topic through the seated
advisors' lenses, sourced to real dossiers, with no invented citations.

## Setup

Board profile at Boardroom hostility level (default). No pre-existing
session briefs required.

## Input

```text
/board get me smart on answer-engine optimization
```

## Pass criteria

- [ ] Posture is detected and confirmed as **Brief me** before the board
      convenes.
- [ ] `wren-halley` (Innovation & Foresight, incl. AI) is among the seated
      advisors.
- [ ] The synthesis is structured as a briefing with all four required
      sub-sections: what matters, what's contested, what to watch, and
      sources.
- [ ] Every source cited traces to an actual dossier under `dossiers/`
      (e.g. a Wren Halley take citing `paul-roetzer.md` or
      `christopher-penn.md`) — no invented reports, studies, or citations
      that don't map to a real dossier or a seat's documented positions.
- [ ] Where genuine disagreement exists among the seated advisors' sources
      on the topic, it appears under "what's contested" rather than being
      smoothed into one voice.
